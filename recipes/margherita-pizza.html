Margherita Pizza

This Italian classic needs no introduction! Classic Margherita Pizza is a perfect amalgamation of cheesy, creamy sauce topped with a melange of spices and herbs. Baked to perfection, this classic Pizza can be prepared easily at home that too without putting in much efforts. For all those who believe that veggies break the 'cheesy' harmony of pizza, Classic Margherita Pizza is a delectable saviour. However, instead of going for the rubbery, store bought pizza, you can try this easy Margherita Pizza recipe at home and impress your loved one with your culinary skills.

Ingredients

  • 1 large pizza base
  • 140 gm grated mozzarella
  • 2 gm basil
  • 2 ml olive oil pomace
  • 50 ml pizza sauce
  • 60 gm sliced tomato
  • 1 gm salt
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven

    Pre-heat oven at 250 degree Celsius. Spread tomato ketchup, fresh basil along with grated mozzarella cheese at the top of pizza base, and garnish with sliced tomatoes.

  2. Spread the sauce and add cheese

    Spread tomato ketchup, fresh basil along with grated mozzarella cheese at the top of pizza base, and top it with sliced tomatoes.

  3. Let it bake

    Bake in a pre-heated oven at 10 to 12 minutes and glaze with olive oil and basil.

  4. Serve hot and relish!

    Serve hot when its done. Sprinkle some oregano and chilli flakes, and relish the taste of this yummy pizza!
