Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish consisting of a spicy mixture of mashed vegetables served with buttered pav (bread rolls). It's a flavorful delight loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, cauliflower), finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 pav (bread rolls)
  • Butter for toasting pav

Steps

  1. Cook the vegetables

    In a pressure cooker, cook mixed vegetables until soft. Mash them coarsely.

  2. Prepare the bhaji

    Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and onions. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
    Add tomatoes and cook until they soften. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
    Add the mashed vegetables and capsicum. Mix thoroughly.

  3. Simmer and garnish

    Let the bhaji simmer for 10-15 minutes. Garnish with coriander leaves and a dollop of butter.

  4. Toast the pav

    Slice the pav rolls horizontally. Toast them on a griddle with butter until golden brown.

  5. Serve hot

    Serve the hot and spicy bhaji with buttered pav along with chopped onions, lemon wedges, and a side of green chili.
