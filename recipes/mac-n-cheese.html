Macaroni and Cheese

Macaroni and Cheese is a classic comfort food dish loved by many. It's a creamy and cheesy pasta dish that's simple to make and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni

    Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the cheese sauce

    In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add flour and whisk until smooth. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
    Reduce heat, add shredded cheddar cheese, salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.

  3. Combine macaroni and cheese sauce

    Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce. Mix until the macaroni is evenly coated with the cheese sauce.

  4. Bake (optional)

    Transfer the macaroni and cheese mixture to a baking dish. Top with breadcrumbs if desired. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.

  5. Serve hot

    Serve the creamy and cheesy macaroni and cheese immediately as a delightful comfort meal!
